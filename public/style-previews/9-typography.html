Typography is the voice of your content. Body 16–18px, line length 45–75 characters, line height 1.5–1.7. Headlines need weight and presence; body needs comfort for sustained reading.

Pair typefaces with clear contrast: serif for headlines, sans for UI; or one family with weight variation. Test at actual sizes.
